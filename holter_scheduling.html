Holter Monitor:

If holter is not placed while patient is in clinic, always offer to mail a monitor to their house as a first option. This is the easiest method for schedulers, technicians and assistants as it reduces paperwork, doesn't require a scheduled appointment and doesn't require time dedicated towards installation.

If the patient agrees, ask them how long the doctor wants them to wear the monitor and send this information along with the patient's MRN to Michael Owen or Jennifer Rashid via Epic Secure Chat or Teams. If they don't know, we will default the duration to 7 days.

The monitor will ship with intuitive instructions that allow the patient to install the monitor themselves and there will be a book containing answers to the most commonly asked questions such as showering, exercising, monitor falling off etc.

The box will also contain a USPS prepaid shipping label for mailing the monitor back after use.

If patient instead wishes for clinic staff to place the monitor, use any open slot on the AUHC EKG schedule (from an active order). The visit type is "Holter/Event."

Please aim to schedule these during times most of the cardiology staff can be present (currently Tuesday mornings, Thursdays and Friday afternoons).

Other times are acceptable but can present challenges due to the differences in staff availability and the management of multiple schedules which can potentially overlap.
